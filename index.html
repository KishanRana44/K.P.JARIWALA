About Us

K.P. Jariwala is a family-owned business specializing in high-quality jari threads for traditional and modern embroidery. With decades of experience, we bring the richness of Indian craftsmanship to your designs.

Our Products

German Silver Jari Thread

High-quality silver thread for elegant designs.

Imitation Jari Thread

Vibrant colored threads for modern embroidery.

Our Services

We offer custom embroidery services,bulk orders and consultations for designers.Let us help you bring your creative visions to life.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To preserve and promote the art of traditional embroidery by providing the finest quality jari threads to artisans and designers worldwide.

Vision: To become the global leader in jari craftsmanship, blending tradition with innovation to create timeless designs.
